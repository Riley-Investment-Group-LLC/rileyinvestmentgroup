# Riley Investment Group LLC - Business Description

## Refined Professional Description

**Riley Investment Group LLC** is a wholesale distributor of enterprise computer hardware and components. We supply hard drives, graphics processors, and server equipment to businesses nationwide.

---

## What NOT to Include

❌ Don't mention: "authorized distributors" (sounds like you're justifying)
❌ Don't mention: "manufacturers" (sounds defensive)
❌ Don't mention: specific product models (H200, 5090, etc.)
❌ Don't mention: "across the United States" (sounds like you're trying too hard)
❌ Don't mention: "system integrators, data centers" (too specific, sounds defensive)

---

## What TO Include

✅ Simple: "Wholesale distributor"
✅ Clear: "Computer hardware"
✅ Professional: "B2B" or "Enterprise"
✅ Confident: Short and direct
