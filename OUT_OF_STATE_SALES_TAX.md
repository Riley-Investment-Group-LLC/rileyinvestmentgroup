## 📋 SALES TAX RULES BY CUSTOMER LOCATION

### Indiana Customers

**Taxable** (charge 7% tax):
- Customer in Indiana
- No resale certificate
- End user

**Tax-Exempt** (charge $0 tax):
- Customer in Indiana
- Has ST-105 resale certificate
- Reselling products

---

### Out-of-State Customers (Wyoming, etc.)

**Generally NO tax** ✅:
- Customer NOT in Indiana
- You don't have nexus in their state
- You don't collect their state's tax
- Invoice: $0 tax

**Exception** (rare):
- If you have physical presence in their state
- If you have employees in their state
- If you have warehouse in their state
- **You don't have any of these** ✅
