## 💡 UNDERSTANDING ST-105 RESALE CERTIFICATES

### What is ST-105?

**Indiana ST-105 Resale Certificate** = Proof customer is buying to resell

**Who has one**:
- ✅ Retailers
- ✅ Wholesalers
- ✅ Distributors
- ✅ Anyone with an Indiana Registered Retail Merchant Certificate (TID)

**Who doesn't**:
- ❌ End users (using products themselves)
- ❌ Companies buying for internal use
- ❌ Non-resellers

### Why You Need It

**With ST-105 on file**:
- ✅ You don't charge them tax
- ✅ They're responsible for tax when they resell
- ✅ You're protected from DOR audit
- ✅ You report as tax-exempt sale on ST-103

**Without ST-105**:
- ❌ You MUST charge them 7% tax
- ❌ You're responsible for collecting and remitting
- ❌ If you don't collect, YOU owe the tax
